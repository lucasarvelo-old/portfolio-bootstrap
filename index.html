Pomodoro Timer

This is a beautiful Pomodoro timer where you can keep track of your sessions and breaks directly from the browser.
At the end of each session, an alarm will let you now that your break starts and at the end of the break, another alarm will let you know when come back to work. You can customize your sessions and breaks length and also use your keyboard to start/pause the timer or reset to the default setting.
